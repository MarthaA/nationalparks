We were both attending Kent State University when we started dating. Our favorite dates were when we went to Cuyahoga Valley National Park in Peninsula, Ohio. Two of our favorite trails are Ledges and Brandywine Falls. We enjoy this park so much that we got our engagement photos here and later got married at Hines Hill in the park.
