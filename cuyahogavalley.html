When we started dating we went on hikes, a lot. Maybe because we were college students attending Kent State University but mostly because we are nature enthusiasts we love going on dates out in nature. Our favorite spots in Cuyahoga Valley National Park are The Ledges and Brandywine Falls. Currently, we live in Cleveland, Ohio.

We love Cuyahoga Valley National Park so much that we had our engagement photos taken there. A year later we even got married in the park. Throughout our engagement we talked about how much we wanted to see Yellowstone National Park. It was fitting that took our honeymoon there eight months later. The National Parks have been such an important part of our relationship that we made it our goal to visit all 59 in our lifetime.
